# Hook-first analysis overhaul eval

## Eval video
**Scenario:** beginner talking-head TikTok about baby naps.

- First frame: creator standing still in flat bedroom lighting
- Opening line: "hey guys, today i wanted to talk about naps because i get this question a lot"
- Captions are readable
- CTA at the end says "follow for more baby sleep tips"
- Runtime: 34s

## Before (representative old-style summary)
> decent info, but the CTA is too generic and your caption strategy could be stronger. add a more specific follow CTA, tighten your hashtags, and keep your captions higher on screen. the hook could be better too.

**Why this was weak:**
- Hook failure is buried as one issue among many
- Late-video CTA/caption feedback gets equal billing even though most viewers never reach it
- It does not teach the creator *why* distribution dies early
- No concrete hook rewrite beyond "could be better"

## After (hook-first target output)
> your first 2-3 seconds are why this video stalls. opening with "hey guys, today i wanted to talk about naps" is a generic warm-up, not a hook, so tiktok likely sees early swipes in the first test batch and never gives your helpful advice a real push. the captions are actually fine, but that barely matters if strangers leave before the value starts.
>
> fix the opener first: start with **"if your baby fights every nap, you're probably doing this one thing too early"** while moving closer to camera in frame 1 and putting bold text on screen: **STOP DOING THIS BEFORE NAP TIME**. that gives you audience call-out + problem promise + visual movement immediately.
>
> once the hook earns the hold, then worry about polishing the end CTA.

**Why this is better:**
- Hook becomes the clear primary diagnosis
- Explicitly deprioritizes CTA work until the opening is fixed
- Explains likely early distribution failure in beginner language
- Gives exact rewrite guidance for the spoken line and text overlay

## Ship check
This is the bar the new prompt + scoring + roast page structure are designed to hit whenever the hook score lands in the weak range.
